About Us

Our Story...

We started in April 2002, initiated by Mr. Inder Kapoor. We are a brand focusing on raw as well as fresh ready to eat/cook meat products. The quality of meat is beyond the standard quality. We cater to all tastes in our selection of food. Moreover, we are fuelled by a team of food specialists who hunt down the best and freshest spices and ingredients from around the world.

Our secret to great food.

Our priority is authenticity and purity. We make you experience the taste, texture, and tenderness of the farm. Fresh meat delivered straight from our own high-quality farms. you can be sure that the meat on your table comes from a healthy animal raised in a hygienic and healthy environment. We have an endless selection to let you have your pick.

Get our products delivered to your doorstep.

We are available for online orders 24/7 and 365 days a year.
We have many other franchises in cities like Guelph, Surrey, Brampton, and many more.
